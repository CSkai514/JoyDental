Braces

What is Braces?

Braces treatment is used to improve the appearance and function of the patient's teeth and/or jaw by using pressure, usually applied through orthodontic devices such as braces, clear aligners, retainers, and orthodontic headgear.

  • 01

    Our dentists always begin an appropriate treatment plan through an initial consultation, talking to you about your individual wishes and needs.

    This is followed by a complete assessment, including medical imaging, to allow us to formulate a specific treatment plan for you.

  • 02

    After the treatment plan is agreed upon, the dentist will begin the fitting process .

    This involves taking a digital 3D mold / scan of your teeth to create a device that fits you.

  • 03

    Throughout the treatment period, the dentist will continue to monitor your progress.

    The dentist will make any necessary adjustments or changes based on the treatment plan.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Although it is recommended that orthodontics be considered from the age of 7, adults can also receive this treatment well.

Braces are a long-term treatment, and can take anywhere from 6 months to 30 months depending on the individual case. Most cases will take around 18 months.

We offer several braces options, depending on the needs of each individual. These include:

  • Traditional metal braces
  • Invisalign
  • Brackets are clear
  • Ceramic braces
  • Self-ligating braces / Damon Braces

Since braces are devices that are in your mouth for a long time, you need to brush your teeth at least 3 times a day to remove any food that may be stuck between the braces, wires, and brackets. Patients are also advised to floss to reach areas of the mouth/teeth that cannot be reached by a toothbrush.
